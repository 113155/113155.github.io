My life story

My name is Deborah Igaba, born and raised in a town called Kampala found in Uganda. I lived in Kampala all my life until I moved to the United States of America after my college early last year. I lived in Centreville,Virginia for about a year and a half before I moved to Fairfeild, Iowa for school.

Life in America has granted me lots of oppotunities in terms of career and studies. More so, I have gotten to meet people from all parts of the world with different cultures and backgrounds. I hope to use these oppotunities to give back to the community and restore hope in the lives of people around me through writing code.
